About
Meghan

I'm a Front-End Developer with a passion for continuous learning! I love puzzles and coming up with creative solutions to problems, so I face coding challenges with excitement. I have a background in English Literature, which has given me an eye for syntax and the ability to perform self-directed research when presented with a challenge. (Translation: I know how to write highly effective Google searches!)

I'm super excited about web development because it allows me to combine logic and creativity to create fun projects. I love exploring new ways of creating web applications with new methods and frameworks. When I'm not working on larger projects, you can find me stretching my skills with creative projects like CSS animations!

When I'm not coding, I enjoy reading and talking about comics, playing board games, and hanging out with my two dogs.
